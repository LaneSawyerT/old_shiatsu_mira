About Me:

“I am trained as a Shiatsu Therapist and graduated from the International School of Shiatsu, Microbiotic Philosophy, in Italy with a Master's degree in 2011 and I am part of the Italian Shiatsu Federation.



I have been researching the connection between emotions and thoughts, and how they effect our physical health since a young age. I then attended a short course in my teenage years, and when I saw the effects of studying the basics I decided to progress on to completing my Masters over a four year period in Shiatsu.



I regularly attend courses and workshops internationally as part of my personal development and my passion has become my profession.
